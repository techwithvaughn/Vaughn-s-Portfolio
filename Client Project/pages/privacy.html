1. Introduction

Joshua Haye & Co (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone) and business identifiers required for services.
  • Financial and engagement information you provide to us or authorize us to obtain.
  • Communications with us, including emails and form submissions.

3. How We Use Information

  • To deliver accounting, audit, tax, and advisory services.
  • To communicate with you and manage engagements.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • To improve our services and website.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

We process personal data on the basis of contract (service delivery), legal obligation (statutory compliance), legitimate interests (service improvement and client management), and consent where required.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers under contract (e.g., IT, secure communications), professional advisers, or regulators where required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Retention

We retain personal data as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and to meet legal, regulatory, and professional requirements.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Restriction or objection to certain processing.
  • Withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent.

9. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

10. International Transfers

If we transfer data internationally, we will use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable laws.
